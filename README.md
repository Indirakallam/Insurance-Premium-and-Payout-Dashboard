Insurance Premium & Payout KPI Dashboard
Overview
This project presents a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze insurance premiums, claims, payouts, and loss ratios. It demonstrates how raw insurance data can be transformed into business‑ready insights that support underwriting, risk assessment, and financial decision‑making.

Project Purpose
The goal of this dashboard is to provide a clear, data‑driven view of insurance performance metrics. It enables stakeholders to monitor premium trends, evaluate claim patterns, assess risk categories, and identify areas that require strategic attention.

Tech Stack
Power BI Desktop – Data modeling, DAX, interactive reporting

Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

Excel/CSV Dataset – Insurance premium and claims data

Business Analysis Techniques – KPI definition, domain understanding, dashboard storytelling

Data Source
Publicly available Insurance Premium and Claims dataset containing:

Customer demographics

Policy details

Premium amounts

Claim amounts

Payout status

Risk categories

Business Impact
This dashboard enables insurance teams to:

Monitor premium collection and claim payouts in real time

Identify high‑risk segments and loss‑making policy categories

Improve underwriting decisions through data‑driven insights

Detect rising claim trends early to reduce financial exposure

Support executive reporting with clear KPIs and visual storytelling
